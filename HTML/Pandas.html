How to look a Panda and what did they

Pandas are an endangered species and a global conservation symbol. Native to China, they inhabit mountainous regions with bamboo forests. Despite being carnivores, 99% of their diet consists of bamboo. Recognizable by their distinctive black and white fur, pandas spend much of their time foraging for food or resting. These solitary creatures can weigh up to 150 kilograms despite their herbivorous diet, captivating people worldwide with their adorable appearance and unique lifestyle

Things about Pandas

Things Pandas love:

  • Bambu:It is their main food and primary source of nutrition.
  • Enrichment toys: They enjoy playing with simple or interactive toys that provide them with entertainment and mental stimulation.
  • Water:Some pandas like to play in water or simply cool off in it.
  • Rest: They enjoy resting and sleeping, as pandas are known to be quite relaxed.
  • Exploration: They have innate curiosity, so they enjoy exploring their environment, even if it's in a calm manner.

Now things Pandas hates >:c

  • Loud noises: Pandas are sensitive to loud noises, which can startle or disturb them.
  • Disruption of routine: Pandas prefer a calm and predictable environment, so sudden changes or disruptions in their routine can cause stress.
  • Conflict or aggression: They are generally peaceful animals and may become distressed in situations involving conflict or aggression.
  • Unfamiliar or crowded spaces: Pandas might feel uncomfortable or stressed in unfamiliar or crowded environments.
  • Unpleasant weather: Extreme temperatures or uncomfortable weather conditions can be distressing for pandas, especially if it affects their habitat or comfort.
